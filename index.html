Rules of the game

Two players simultaneously choose one of three hand gestures: rock, paper, or scissors.

Rock crushes scissors, scissors cut paper, and paper covers rock.

If both players choose the same gesture, it's a tie, and they play again.

The first player to win two out of three rounds wins the game.
